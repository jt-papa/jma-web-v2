Our Process
01
Discovery

Let’s talk. Whether you’re working with a specific theme, budget, or target audience, give us what you’ve got and we’ll take it from there. We know the right questions to ask to get to the heart of your request.

02
Ideation

Once we have identified your goals, we’ll tap into our network of over 3,000 suppliers and 40+ years of experience. We’ll provide an interactive presentation of product ideas that suit your message and price range with your brand’s logo, colors, artwork and style in mind.

03
Collaboration

Now, we listen. After sharing our ideas with you, we’re all ears for feedback! We work with you to narrow down options, identify key details of interest and provide additional materials to help with your decision.

04
Activation

This is the fun part. As soon as you give the go-ahead, we are meticulously hands-on through proofing, production and delivery. We can’t wait to get your merchandise into the hands of your team and your clients.

05
Connectivity

If you are showcasing your products in a corporate program, our tech team will build you a custom, user friendly website that aligns with your brand. We’ll handle any necessary integrations, too. We have expertise with various purchasing systems like ARIBA and Coupa.

06
Launch

Time to bring your custom site to life! We make it easier than ever for your team to access and purchase company merch. We’ll oversee your ongoing program to maximize utility, look for efficiencies and continuously enhance your offerings.
